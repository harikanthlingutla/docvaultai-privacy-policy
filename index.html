Privacy Policy for DocVaultAI

Effective Date: March 13, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to DocVaultAI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

Who We Are

DocVaultAI is a document management application developed and maintained by Harikanth Lingutla. We can be contacted at support@docvaultai.com.

Data We Collect

Data Stored on Your Device

DocVaultAI is designed with privacy in mind. The documents and information you process in our app are stored locally on your device and are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods. We do not have access to these documents or their contents.

We collect and store the following information:

  • Document Images: Photos or scans of documents you add to the app
  • Extracted Text: Text extracted from your documents using OCR technology
  • Document Metadata: Information such as document type, date added, and custom tags
  • App Settings: Your preferences and settings within the app

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app, including:

  • Feature usage statistics
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports

This data is anonymized and cannot be linked to your identity.

Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, Apple App Store handles all payment processing. We receive information about your subscription status but do not have access to your payment details.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve and optimize the app
  • To detect and address technical issues
  • To communicate with you about app updates or changes

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your documents are encrypted on your device using industry-standard encryption algorithms.

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

We use Apple App Store for distribution and in-app purchases. Apple privacy policy applies to the information collected through the App Store. You can view Apple privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools to help us understand how users interact with our app. These tools collect anonymous usage data as described above.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to delete your data
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@docvaultai.com.

Children Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the effective date.

Contact Us
